Payment and Cancellation Policies

To maintain client privacy, we do not directly bill insurance companies. Your doctor will not discuss your care with insurance providers and will not provide insurance companies, or any third-party payers, access to your diagnosis or treatment records. However, many clients with private insurance do choose to have their insurance partially reimburse them for our services. Should you request this, we will provide you with a billing invoice that you may submit to your insurance provider, who may reimburse you for our services at your policyâ€™s out-of-network rate.

Medicare and Medicaid?

Unfortunately, we cannot currently provide care for Medicare or Medicaid patients.

Intake Paperwork

For patients unable to fill out paperwork prior to appointment, please arrive thirty minutes early to do so.

Can't Make Your Appointment?

If you are unable to make your appointment it is important that you call and let us know. Patients who are 15 minutes late or more for an appointment may be rescheduled for another time.
