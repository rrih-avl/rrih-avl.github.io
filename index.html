Our mission is to reach people where they are and to provide them with compassionate and holistic medical care. We treat the person, not just the disease, so that they can have healthy, meaningful and productive lives.

Who We Are

Reach Recovery and Integrated Health, a private-pay medical services provider, is located in the scenic mountains of Western North Carolina in the charming town of Weaverville. We offer kind, non-judgmental, comprehensive healthcare to meet the needs of our patients. We practice evidence-based medicine following the American Society of Addiction Medicine guidelines for our patients who suffer from substance use disorder.

Medication Assisted Recovery

Addiction is a primary brain disease that affects the dopamine reward centers in the brain. It cannot be overcome by willpower or counseling alone. Substance use disorder has both psychological and physical components and patients often suffer a severe disconnect from self, society, and family. We provide medication assisted therapy, personalized medical care as well as intentional recovery education. Our objective is meaningful change through the process of neural re-patterning, we invite patients and family members to become involved in educational groups led by Certified Peer Recovery Specialists.

Concierge Healthcare

Patients want to feel better, to be heard, and to have their time respected. Concierge medicine fulfills these expectations. By limiting our membership, we are able to offer comprehensive, personalized healthcare (to qualified patients) at an affordable price, especially for those without insurance coverage or with high deductible plans.
